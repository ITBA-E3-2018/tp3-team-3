\chapter{Ejercicio 1}

The inputs of the system are $I$ and $S$, and the outputs are $B_1$ and $B_2$. If $B_1=1$ pump 1 is turned on, and if $B_2$ pump 1 is turned off. The same relation exists between $B_2$ and pump 2.
